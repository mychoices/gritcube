Gritcube envisions an entrepreneurial Africa filled with job opportunities and economic growth.

Our Story

Gritcube started in the summer of 2015 by four individuals who have a shared passion to contribute to the economic growth of African countries through the development and diversification of its private sectors.

Our Team

The founders come from various backgrounds in social science research, environment development, marketing, and data analysis. Combined with the director of business and local African program managers, they form the head and heart of Gritcube.
